Agency History

Prestige Wheels Car Rentals, founded in 2005 by car enthusiasts Alex Reynolds and Sarah Mitchell, has evolved from a small startup with just five vehicles to a thriving car rental agency with a presence in multiple cities across the state. Fueled by their commitment to exceptional customer service, Prestige Wheels expanded its fleet and integrated cutting-edge technology to streamline the rental process. Over the years, the agency has become synonymous with quality and convenience. In 2020, they took a step towards sustainability by introducing eco-friendly vehicles, demonstrating their dedication to both their customers and the environment. Today, Prestige Wheels Car Rentals stands as a beacon of excellence in the car rental industry, serving the needs of travelers and locals alike
